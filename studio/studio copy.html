Lettering

A Land of Paradox

This design captures Myanmar's conflicting realities. The two opposing arrows symbolize the nation's agonizing stalemate and deep conflict: the forceful push and pull between hope and oppression. It visualizes a country caught in a desperate deadlock.

Straw Fire

This design explores the fleeting nature of human vice or short-lived enthusiasm (a "straw fire"). It symbolizes intense, destructive energy that flares up brightly but quickly dies out, highlighting temporary outbursts of bad behavior or passion without lasting substance.

Single

This design celebrates the power of solitude and independence. It connects being "Single" to absolute freedom—the unburdened state of making your own path. It embodies self-reliance, the clarity of an undivided focus, and the quiet strength found in personal, limitless choice.

Love's Origin

A lettering artwork expressing the gentle beginning of affection. Each curve and stroke flows with warmth and emotion, symbolizing how love quietly takes root and blossoms from the heart.
